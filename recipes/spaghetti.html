Spaghetti

Description:

This is a delightful family recipe that will warm any heart.

You and your familiy will LOVE it!

Ingredients:

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce)jar spaghetti
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 pound shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes; drain.
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  4. In a large skillet over medium heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain.
  5. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes.
  6. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarrela, cheddar and ricotta; stir well.
  7. In a 9 x 13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  8. Bake in a preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
