Spaghetti

Description:

This is a wonderful, quick & easy recipe that will please everyone.

You and your familiy will LOVE it!

Ingredients:

  • 1 (16 ounce) package spaghetti noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (32 ounce)jar meatless spaghetti sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 5 tablespons butter, melted
  • 2 cups small curd cottage cheese, divided
  • 5 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Lightly grease a 9 x 13-inch baking dish.
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  4. Cook pasta in boiling water, stirring occaisionally, until tender, about 12 minutes; drain.
  5. Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
  6. Cook and stir beef and onion in hot skillet until meat is browed and onion is translucent, about 7 minutes.
  7. Drain excess oil from the pan.
  8. Stir in spaghetti sauce and seasoned salt.
  9. Whisk eggs, parmesan, and butter in a large bowl.
  10. Add spaghetti and toss to coat.
  11. Place 1/2 of the spaghetti mixture into the prepared dish.
  12. Top with 1/2 of the cottage cheese, 2 cups mozzarella, and 1/2 of meat sauce. Repeat in layers.
  13. Cover with aluminum foil.
  14. Bake in a preheated oven for 40 minutes
  15. Remove the foil and sprinkle with remaining mozzarella.
  16. Continue to bake until cheese is melted and lightly browned, about 20 to 25 minutes
